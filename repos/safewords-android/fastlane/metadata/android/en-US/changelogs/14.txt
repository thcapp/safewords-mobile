v1.3.0 — Verification primitives.

- Plain Mode is now the default home screen. The big-word, big-button layout is the front door.
- New: static override word (deterministic, printable), challenge/answer table, and 6-digit numeric code format. Configure under Settings → Verification.
- Cross-platform contract: same primitives produce the same words on iOS.
- Tap the "Standard view" pill in the top-right of Plain home to switch to the tabbed Advanced UI; the choice is sticky.
